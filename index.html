Our Services

“PLEXUS RETIREMENT RESORTS” is the first service provider in Sri Lanka which provides “Three-In-One” service to elders.

24-hours Assisted Living

Our 24-hour Assisted Living community provides unparalleled support and comfort, ensuring that residents receive the care they need at any time of day or night. With compassionate, professional staff available around the clock

Independent Living

Our Independent Living community is designed for active seniors seeking a maintenance-free lifestyle. Residents enjoy the privacy of their own apartments or cottages, complemented by access to amenities like dining options

Visitors Cottage

Experience comfort and charm in our Visitor’s Cottage, perfect for short getaways or extended stays. Nestled in a serene setting, the cottage features cozy living spaces, a fully equipped kitchen.

Restaurant & Community Hall

Our Restaurant & Community Hall offers a perfect blend of delicious dining and versatile event space. The restaurant serves a diverse menu in a warm and inviting atmosphere, ideal for casual meals or special occasions.

Facilities

There are plenty of facilities available at our residencies with hospitality and dedicated human kind.

Independent_Living
Designed for individuals who value their freedom and self-sufficiency while enjoying a supportive and convenient environment. Whether for seniors, people with disabilities, or those seeking a maintenance-free lifestyle.
Elders Accomodation
Designed with care and comfort in mind, our elder accommodation provides a safe and welcoming environment for senior guests. Each space is thoughtfully furnished with easy-access features.
Visitors Cottage
Nestled in a tranquil setting, our cozy visitor cottage is your perfect retreat. With its rustic charm and modern amenities, this cottage offers a warm and inviting space for relaxation. It features a comfortable living area.
Transportation
Our transport services offer a seamless travel experience with a fleet of well-maintained vehicles and professional drivers. Whether you need local transfers, long-distance travel, or special event transportation.
Spiritual Center

Community Center

Restaurant
